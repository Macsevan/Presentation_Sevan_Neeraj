Below are real screenshots of our Streamlit web app that was developed and deployed to provide real-time car price predictions based on user input.

⚙️ Input Forms

The form dynamically collects required information — car brand, year, fuel type, kilometers driven, and more.

Dropdowns and sliders enhance user experience and prevent incorrect inputs.

Input validation and formatting ensure that the model receives clean data for accurate predictions.

Streamlit's clean layout helps guide users through each required field step-by-step.

📥 Submitted Form and Prediction Output

Once the user submits the form, the trained model predicts the car's value. The result is shown in a styled card with currency formatting.
